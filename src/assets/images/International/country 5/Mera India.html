India Tour & Holiday Packages

A kaleidoscope of traditions, culture, and vibrant geographies, India speaks for itself as a soul-stirring journey. Snow-covered peaks of the Himalayas stretch of coastline, natural greenery to depths of spirituality, and clusters of cultural shades. All of these aspects join together to define the raw beauty through which India captures the heart of every tourist. Discover the different facets of this multicolored country as it shapes your vision at every fold. With the country's tourism branched into several forms, India has a chunk for every kind of traveler. To get things going, here is an India travel planner which incorporates must-visit tourist attractions, places to visit in India, how to reach, and the best time to visit.

Exchange your sunny days with breezy happiness at hill stations

Just the idea of being surrounded by the mighty mountains, snowcap on or off, is a dream. Going on a vacation to hill stations in India is a blissful experience. Numerous destinations from north to south and east to west that quench your thirst for a perfect escape from summers. India has a lot in store for its travelers, from trying adventure activities, sitting in solace, feeling the joy of shopping, and meeting wild creatures. Some top picks are Srinagar, Nainital, Manali, Shimla, Mussoorie, Ooty, Munnar, Matheran, and Kodaikanal.

Exploring the wild: Is your camera focused on the point?

India possesses a striking diversity when it comes to wildlife. Often called the other half, the wild creatures have their presence marked in different states of the nation. With over 400 wildlife sanctuaries, 89 national parks, and 13 bio-reserves, India is best known for having the richest biodiversity in the world. Take your anticipation a level up at Kaziranga National Park, Assam; Periyar National Park, Kerala; Sasan Gir National Park, Corbett, Ranthambore, and Bandhavgarh. These are some of the top national parks in India where thrill and adventure run hand in hand. Royal Bengal Tigers, Indian elephants, Asiatic Lions, and 2/3rd of the Great Single-horned Rhinoceros are the significant species that add one reason for travelers to visit India. Along with them, the motherland sings a lullaby to 400 species of mammals, 1250 birds, 2550 fishes, 460 reptiles, 240 amphibians, and 2550 fishes. Tiger safari in India is a rare thrill. Wildlife safari tours are among the most sought options of Indian travel as the heart of nature is kept intact and even preserved.

Goosebumps worthy adventure

Blame it on the mountains, the topsy turvy roads, or the rushing water, as they are the ones who keep on calling for an adventure getaway. Of all the things India is known for, the adrenaline rush of trying thrilling activities is something that spices life up. Whether you like river rafting, trekking, camping, waterfall rappelling, skydiving, caving, or hot air ballooning, India has a spot to try each one of these activities. But, wait, the list isn't over yet! Heli-skiing, scuba diving, cliff jumping, paragliding, trekking, snorkeling, and mountain biking are other exhilarating experiences in India. Further, tourist attractions in India that echo all this madness are Rishikesh, Manali, Thane, Goa, Andaman, Deesa, Meghalaya, Zanskar, Ladakh, and Bir Billing. So bounce away from the monotony that stops you and go to places in India that refresh your soul.
